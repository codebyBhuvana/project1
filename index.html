Kanyadaanam

This is the emotional moment when the bride's parents give away their daughter to the groom, entrusting him with her well-being. It signifies the transfer of responsibility and is considered a highly sacred act in Hindu weddings.

Jeelakarra Bellam

The bride and groom place a paste made of jeelakarra (cumin seeds) and bellam (jaggery) on each other's heads. This act symbolizes the inseparable bond and the sweet and enduring nature of their relationship.

Saptapadi (Seven Steps)

The couple takes seven sacred steps around the holy fire, each step representing a vow they make to each other. It is the most crucial part of the wedding, signifying the spiritual and physical union of the bride and groom.
